Meet Our Directors

Our Directors

Arifa Mohamed

Arifa Mohamed is an accomplished professional with a diverse background in marketing, law and business development.


Arifa holds a Degree in Law and is currently the CEO of SocialRank, where her leadership has been instrumental in the company’s establishment as a prominent player in the marketing landscape, serving clients around the world.


Concurrently, Arifa also serves as the Business Development Manager for ACE Consultancy Inc, a role that allowed her to harness her marketing expertise while forging strategic partnerships and expanding the company's market presence.


Beyond her professional pursuits, she actively participates in social causes and encourages her teams to engage in philanthropic endeavours, making a positive impact on society.

Roger Bissessar

Roger Bissessar is a Technical Services Specialist with over twenty-five (25) years of experience in execution of Global Projects.


Roger holds a BSc – Chemical / Process Engineering and a Diploma – Project Management. He has worked with Multi-National and National companies on projects from design stage through to construction, commissioning, start-up and operations.


His extensive experience in the energy sector includes project execution for two (2) world scale methanol plants, four (4) offshore platforms, commissioning of natural gas pipelines from offshore to onshore, optimization of petrochemical plants and onshore oil processing facilities and loading terminal.


Roger is involved in various NGOs promoting youth development and entrepreneurship. He serves as a Mentor for Youth Business Caribbean and is involved in STEM training initiatives for Primary and Secondary schools. He is passionate about Wellness initiatives and supports various networks for promoting Mental Wellness.
