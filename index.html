Meet Our Directors

Our Directors

Roger Bissessar

Roger Bissessar is a Technical Services Consultant with over twenty-five (25) years of experience in execution of Global Projects.

Roger holds a BSc – Chemical / Process Engineering and a Diploma – Project Management. He has worked with Multi-National and National companies on projects from design stage through to construction, commissioning, start-up and operations.

His extensive experience in the energy sector includes project execution for two (2) world scale methanol plants, four (4) offshore platforms, commissioning of natural gas pipelines from offshore to onshore, optimization of petrochemical plants and onshore oil processing facilities and loading terminal.

Roger is involved in various NGOs promoting youth development and entrepreneurship. He serves as a Mentor for Youth Business Caribbean and is involved in STEM training initiatives for Primary and Secondary schools. He is passionate about Wellness initiatives and supports various networks for promoting Mental Wellness.

Dr. Rosh Khan

Rosh Khan is the Managing Director of SocialRank Media, Guyana’s leading marketing agency, and the President of the Masterclass Institute, a business development firm and Guyana’s only approved Covey Training Center.

After graduating with a Medical Degree (MD) at 23, he dived into the world of entrepreneurship by starting a series of small businesses. His digital marketing firm, SocialRank Media, has since evolved into a global agency, serving thousands of businesses around the world.

Through the Masterclass Institute, he also became the youngest facilitator in the region with the world-renowned FranklinCovey organization. Rosh is also the recipient of two awards: The Young Business Executive of the Year Award and the National Youth Award for Entrepreneurship.

He also served as the Curator of the Global Shapers – Georgetown Hub, through which, he was invited to the White House by the Obama Administration.

Rosh believes “We can only achieve national development through people development. We can only build our nation if we build our people."
